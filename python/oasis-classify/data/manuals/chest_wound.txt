Category: Chest Wound / Pneumothorax

STEPS:
1. Call 911 immediately.
2. Seal a sucking chest wound immediately with a chest seal or plastic wrap.
3. Apply 3-sided occlusive seal on exhale: tape three sides, leave one open.
4. Help person into position of easiest breathing — sitting or semi-reclined.
5. If person worsens with seal in place, lift unsealed side to release trapped air.

NEVER DO:
- Do NOT leave a sucking chest wound open and unsealed.
- Do NOT apply a fully airtight seal without a valve — trapped air causes tension pneumothorax.
