Category: Panic Attack

STEPS:
1. Stay calm; speak slowly and reassuringly.
2. Remind them: this feeling is not dangerous and will pass.
3. Coach slow breathing: inhale 4 counts, hold 4, exhale 4, hold 4. Repeat.
4. If breathing too hard, try grounding: name 5 things you see, 4 touch, 3 hear.
5. If chest pain, arm pain, or symptoms last over 20 minutes, call 911.

NEVER DO:
- Do NOT just say calm down -- guide them through breathing instead.
- Do NOT restrain or crowd the person.
