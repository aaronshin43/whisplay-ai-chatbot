Category: Avalanche Survival

STEPS:
1. If caught: discard poles and skis; use swimming motion toward surface and side.
2. As snow slows, cover face with arms and create an air pocket before snow sets.
3. Spit — saliva falls toward down; dig in the opposite direction.
4. Push one arm toward surface as a marker for rescuers.
5. Stay calm, breathe slowly; do not shout unless rescuers are directly above.

NEVER DO:
- Do NOT panic and thrash after snow stops — this compresses your air space rapidly.
- Do NOT dig straight down toward a buried person — approach from below.
