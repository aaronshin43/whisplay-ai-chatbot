Category: Pediatric Emergency

STEPS:
1. Call 911 immediately.
2. Infant CPR (under 1 year): 2 fingers on chest center, compress 4 cm at 100-120/min.
3. Infant rescue breaths: slight head tilt, cover mouth and nose, give small puffs.
4. Child CPR (1 year+): one or two hands, compress one-third chest depth.
5. Infant choking: 5 back blows between shoulder blades, then 5 chest thrusts.

NEVER DO:
- Do NOT perform abdominal thrusts on an infant under 1 year.
- Do NOT give aspirin to a child -- risk of Reye syndrome.
