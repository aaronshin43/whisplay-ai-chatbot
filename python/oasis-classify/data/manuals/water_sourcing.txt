Category: Water Sourcing / Dehydration

STEPS:
1. Recognize dehydration: dark urine, dizziness, dry mouth, rapid heart rate.
2. Moving water (streams) is safer; purify all wild water before drinking.
3. Boil: rolling boil 1 min; above 2000 m altitude, boil 3 minutes.
4. Chemical: iodine or chlorine tablets per instructions; wait 30 min.
5. Severe dehydration: give fluids slowly; call 911 if confused or unconscious.

NEVER DO:
- Do NOT drink seawater -- it accelerates dehydration and damages organs.
- Do NOT drink urine -- it concentrates waste and worsens dehydration.
