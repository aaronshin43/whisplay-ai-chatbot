Category: Lost in Wilderness

STEPS:
1. STOP: stop moving immediately; panic causes poor decisions and burns energy.
2. Think and observe: recall last known position, landmarks, terrain, and supplies.
3. Stay put unless in immediate danger — rescuers search known trails first.
4. Build shelter if night or rain is approaching — exposure kills before dehydration.
5. Signal in groups of three: three whistle blasts, three fires in a triangle.

NEVER DO:
- Do NOT keep moving when lost — you will cover more ground to search.
- Do NOT ignore shelter in favor of food or water — hypothermia is the greatest immediate threat.
