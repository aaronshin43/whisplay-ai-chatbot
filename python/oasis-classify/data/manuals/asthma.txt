Category: Asthma Attack

STEPS:
1. Sit person upright, leaning slightly forward with hands on knees.
2. Help use rescue inhaler (blue, short-acting): shake, place in mouth, press.
3. Give 1 puff every minute up to 10 puffs while waiting for help.
4. Call 911 if no improvement after 10 puffs or breathing worsens.
5. Begin CPR if person becomes unresponsive and stops breathing.

NEVER DO:
- Do NOT lay the person flat during an asthma attack.
- Do NOT substitute a long-acting brown or purple inhaler for the rescue inhaler.
