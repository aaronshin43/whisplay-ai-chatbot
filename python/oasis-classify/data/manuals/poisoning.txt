Category: Poisoning / Toxic Ingestion

STEPS:
1. Call 911 or Poison Control immediately.
2. Identify what was ingested, how much, and when — report to emergency services.
3. Follow specific instructions from Poison Control if person is conscious.
4. If unconscious and breathing, place in recovery position.
5. For skin or eye contact, flush with large amounts of clean water for at least 20 minutes.

NEVER DO:
- Do NOT induce vomiting unless Poison Control specifically instructs you to.
- Do NOT give food, milk, or water to neutralize the poison without professional guidance.
