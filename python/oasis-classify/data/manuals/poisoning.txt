Category: Poisoning / Toxic Ingestion

STEPS:
1. Call 911 or Poison Control immediately.
2. Try to identify what was ingested, how much, and when — tell this to emergency services.
3. If the person is conscious and alert, follow the specific advice from Poison Control.
4. Keep the person calm and still.
5. If the person is unconscious and breathing, place in the recovery position.
6. If the person stops breathing, begin CPR.
7. Bring the container or a sample of the substance with you or describe it clearly to responders.
8. For skin or eye contact with a toxic substance, flush with large amounts of clean water for at least 20 minutes.

NEVER DO:
- Do NOT induce vomiting unless Poison Control specifically instructs you to.
- Do NOT give food, milk, or water to neutralize the poison without professional guidance.
- Do NOT leave the person alone.
- Do NOT induce vomiting if the person is unconscious or the substance was corrosive.
