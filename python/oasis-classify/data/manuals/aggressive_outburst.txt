Category: Aggressive Outburst

STEPS:
1. Prioritize your safety — leave and call 911 if physically threatened.
2. Stay calm; keep voice low, slow, and even.
3. Give physical space; position yourself near an exit.
4. Use short, non-confrontational sentences: I can see you are very upset.
5. Call 911 and move others away if aggression escalates to violence.

NEVER DO:
- Do NOT corner the person or block their exit.
- Do NOT shout, argue, or attempt physical restraint unless trained.
