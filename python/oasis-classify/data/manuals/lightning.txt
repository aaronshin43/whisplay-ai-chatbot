Category: Lightning Strike

STEPS:
1. Confirm the area is safe before approaching — lightning can strike the same location again.
2. Call 911 immediately.
3. It is safe to touch a lightning strike victim — they carry no residual electrical charge.
4. Check for responsiveness and breathing.
5. If not breathing and has no pulse, begin CPR immediately — lightning can cause cardiac arrest.
6. Cover any burns with clean dry dressings.
7. If multiple victims, prioritize the unresponsive who appear dead — lightning victims have a high survival rate with immediate CPR.
8. Keep the victim warm and still until help arrives.

NEVER DO:
- Do NOT delay CPR — rapid resuscitation greatly improves survival.
- Do NOT move the victim unnecessarily if spinal injury is possible.
- Do NOT leave the victim alone.
- Do NOT assume a person struck by lightning cannot survive — begin resuscitation immediately.
