Category: Lightning Strike

STEPS:
1. Confirm area is safe — lightning can strike the same location again.
2. Call 911 immediately. It is safe to touch the victim — no residual charge.
3. Check for responsiveness and breathing.
4. If no pulse and not breathing, begin CPR immediately.
5. Cover burns with clean dry dressings; keep victim warm and still.

NEVER DO:
- Do NOT delay CPR — rapid resuscitation greatly improves survival.
- Do NOT move victim unnecessarily if spinal injury is possible.
