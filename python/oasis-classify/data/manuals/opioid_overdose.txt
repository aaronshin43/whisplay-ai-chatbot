Category: Opioid Overdose

STEPS:
1. Call 911. Signs: unresponsive, slow/stopped breathing, blue lips, pinpoint pupils.
2. Give naloxone (Narcan) -- nasal spray or injection; repeat if no response in 2-3 min.
3. Rescue breathing: 1 breath every 5 sec until naloxone takes effect.
4. When breathing returns, place in recovery position; wait for EMS.

NEVER DO:
- Do NOT leave the person alone -- overdose can return when naloxone wears off.
- Do NOT use cold shower or coffee -- these do not reverse overdose.
