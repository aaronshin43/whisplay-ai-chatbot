Category: Frostbite

STEPS:
1. Move person to a warm environment immediately.
2. Remove wet gloves, socks, or footwear from affected areas gently.
3. Do not rewarm if refreezing is possible — refreezing causes worse damage.
4. Rewarm by immersing in warm water 37-40 degrees C for 20-30 minutes.
5. Cover rewarmed area with dry dressings; pad between frostbitten fingers or toes.

NEVER DO:
- Do NOT rub or massage frostbitten tissue — ice crystals cause further damage.
- Do NOT use fire, stove, or heating pad — uneven heat burns numb tissue.
