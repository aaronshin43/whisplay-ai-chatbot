Category: Seizure / Epilepsy

STEPS:
1. Call 911 if seizure lasts more than 5 minutes or person does not regain consciousness.
2. Clear hard objects away; place something soft under the person's head.
3. Time the seizure from the start.
4. Gently turn person onto their side to keep airway clear.
5. After seizure stops, check breathing; begin CPR if not breathing.

NEVER DO:
- Do NOT put anything in the person's mouth — they cannot swallow their tongue.
- Do NOT hold the person down or restrain them during a seizure.
