Category: Fracture / Broken Bone

STEPS:
1. Call 911 for open fractures, spine/pelvis/femur breaks, or signs of shock.
2. Immobilize the limb as found using a padded rigid splint on both sides.
3. Secure splint above and below the fracture; check pulse/sensation beyond it.
4. Cover open fractures loosely with a clean dressing.
5. Apply ice wrapped in cloth to reduce swelling.

NEVER DO:
- Do NOT attempt to straighten or push bone back into the wound.
- Do NOT apply splint so tightly it cuts off circulation.
