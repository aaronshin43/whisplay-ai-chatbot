Category: Heat Stroke / Heat Exhaustion

STEPS:
1. Call 911 for heat stroke (hot dry skin, confusion, no sweating, high temperature).
2. Move person immediately to a cool shaded or air-conditioned area.
3. Remove excess clothing.
4. Cool rapidly: apply ice packs to neck, armpits, and groin; fan while misting with cool water.
5. If fully conscious and able to swallow, give cool water to drink.

NEVER DO:
- Do NOT delay cooling while waiting for emergency services — start immediately.
- Do NOT give fluids to an unconscious person or one who cannot swallow.
