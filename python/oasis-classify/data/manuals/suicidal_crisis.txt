Category: Suicidal Crisis

STEPS:
1. Stay calm. Do not leave the person alone under any circumstances.
2. Ask directly and without hesitation: Are you thinking about suicide? Asking does not increase risk — it opens the door.
3. Listen without judgment. Acknowledge their pain: I hear that you are in tremendous pain right now.
4. Remove any obvious means from the immediate environment if it is safe to do so.
5. Call 988 (Suicide and Crisis Lifeline) together with the person if possible, or call 911 if there is immediate danger.
6. Do not attempt to resolve the underlying issues. Focus only on keeping them safe right now.
7. If they agree, help them contact a trusted person — a family member, therapist, or friend — to stay with them.

NEVER DO:
- Do NOT leave the person alone at any point.
- Do NOT promise to keep their suicidal thoughts secret.
- Do NOT debate, argue, or challenge their feelings.
- Do NOT minimize their pain with phrases like things could be worse or you have so much to live for.
- Do NOT try to shock or guilt them out of their feelings.
