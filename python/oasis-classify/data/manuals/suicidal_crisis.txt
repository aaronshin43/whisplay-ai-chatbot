Category: Suicidal Crisis

STEPS:
1. Stay calm; do not leave the person alone.
2. Ask directly: "Are you thinking about suicide?" — asking does not increase risk.
3. Listen without judgment; do not minimize their pain.
4. Remove any means (medications, weapons) from reach if safe to do so.
5. Call 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline) together, or 911 if in immediate danger.

NEVER DO:
- Do NOT leave the person alone if there is immediate danger.
- Do NOT promise to keep their suicidal thoughts secret.
