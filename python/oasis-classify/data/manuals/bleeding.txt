Category: Severe Bleeding / Hemorrhage Control

STEPS:
1. Call 911 immediately.
2. Apply direct pressure to wound using clean cloth or bare hands.
3. Press hard — maintain continuous pressure for at least 10 minutes.
4. If blood soaks through, add more material on top without removing first layer.
5. If limb bleeding fails direct pressure, apply tourniquet 5-7 cm above wound.

NEVER DO:
- Do NOT remove an embedded object from the wound — press around it instead.
- Do NOT remove the first cloth layer to inspect — it disrupts clotting.
