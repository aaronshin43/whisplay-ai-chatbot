Category: Stroke

STEPS:
1. Use FAST: Face drooping, Arm weakness, Speech slurred, Time to call 911.
2. Call 911 immediately; note exact time symptoms started — treatment depends on it.
3. Keep person calm, still, and comfortable.
4. If unconscious and breathing, place in recovery position on their side.
5. If not breathing, begin CPR.

NEVER DO:
- Do NOT give aspirin unless emergency services instruct it — stroke may be hemorrhagic.
- Do NOT give food or water — swallowing is often impaired after stroke.
