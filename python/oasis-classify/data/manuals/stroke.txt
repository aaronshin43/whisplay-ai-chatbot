Category: Stroke

STEPS:
1. Use the FAST test: Face drooping, Arm weakness, Speech difficulty, Time to call emergency services.
2. Call emergency services (911) immediately — stroke treatment is time-critical.
3. Note the exact time symptoms started — hospital treatment depends on this.
4. Keep the person calm, still, and comfortable while waiting for help.
5. If unconscious and breathing, place in the recovery position on their side.
6. If not breathing, begin CPR.
7. Loosen any tight clothing around the neck.
8. Do not give the person food, drink, or medication — swallowing is often impaired.

NEVER DO:
- Do NOT give aspirin unless explicitly instructed by emergency services — stroke may be hemorrhagic.
- Do NOT let the person drive themselves to the hospital.
- Do NOT wait to see if symptoms pass on their own — call immediately.
- Do NOT give food or water — the person may not be able to swallow safely.
