Category: Acute Stress Reaction

STEPS:
1. Remove person from ongoing danger before addressing psychological symptoms.
2. Speak slowly using their name; use short, calm sentences.
3. Offer warmth, water, a blanket — physical comfort anchors awareness.
4. Use grounding if dissociated: ask what they can see or feel right now.
5. Call 911 if severely unresponsive or unable to care for themselves.

NEVER DO:
- Do NOT pressure them to describe or relive the traumatic event.
- Do NOT leave someone who is severely dissociated or unresponsive alone.
