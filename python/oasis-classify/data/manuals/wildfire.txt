Category: Wildfire Escape

STEPS:
1. Check wind direction — fire travels fastest uphill and downwind.
2. Move perpendicular to the fire's path to reach an escape route.
3. Protect airway: wet a cloth and hold over nose and mouth to filter smoke.
4. Stay low in heavy smoke — air is cooler and cleaner near the ground.
5. If escape is cut off, find a ditch or cleared area with minimal vegetation; lie face-down, hands protecting face.

NEVER DO:
- Do NOT run uphill toward a wildfire — fire moves uphill faster than you can run.
- Do NOT ignore wind shifts — fire direction can change and cut off escape routes suddenly.
