Category: Burns

STEPS:
1. Remove person from heat source; stop the burning immediately.
2. Cool burn with cool running water for 10-20 minutes.
3. Remove jewelry and loose clothing near burn before swelling occurs.
4. Cover cooled burn with clean non-fluffy material (cling film or clean cloth).
5. Call 911 for burns larger than palm, deep burns, or burns on face or hands.

NEVER DO:
- Do NOT use ice, butter, toothpaste, or oils — they trap heat and increase infection.
- Do NOT pop burn blisters — they protect the wound.
