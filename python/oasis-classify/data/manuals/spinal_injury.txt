Category: Spinal Injury

STEPS:
1. Call 911 immediately. Tell person not to move.
2. Stabilize head: place both hands gently on either side, hold in position found.
3. Do not attempt to straighten the neck or spine.
4. Maintain stabilization until EMS takes over.
5. If unconscious and not breathing, perform jaw thrust without tilting head; begin CPR.

NEVER DO:
- Do NOT move the person unless there is immediate life threat.
- Do NOT tilt the head or attempt to correct any deformity.
