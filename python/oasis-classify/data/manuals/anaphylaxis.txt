Category: Anaphylaxis / Severe Allergic Reaction

STEPS:
1. Call 911 immediately — anaphylaxis is life-threatening.
2. Administer epinephrine auto-injector into the outer thigh without delay.
3. Lay person flat and elevate legs unless breathing is difficult.
4. If breathing is difficult, allow person to sit upright for comfort.
5. Give second epinephrine dose after 5-15 minutes if symptoms persist.

NEVER DO:
- Do NOT delay epinephrine to see if symptoms improve on their own.
- Do NOT use antihistamines as a substitute for epinephrine.
