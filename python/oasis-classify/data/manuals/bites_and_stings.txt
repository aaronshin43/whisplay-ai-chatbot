Category: Bites and Stings

STEPS:
1. Call 911 for anaphylaxis, venomous bite, or multiple stings.
2. Snake bite: keep still, immobilize limb below heart level, remove jewelry.
3. Bee sting: scrape stinger sideways with a card, wash, apply ice.
4. Tick: grasp with fine tweezers near skin, pull straight out steadily.
5. Animal bite: wash with soap and water 5 min; seek medical care.

NEVER DO:
- Do NOT cut and suck venom from a snake bite -- ineffective and dangerous.
- Do NOT crush or squeeze a tick body while removing it.
