Category: Bear Encounter / Bear Attack

STEPS:
1. Do NOT run — running triggers bear's chase instinct.
2. Grizzly attack: fall face-down, cover neck with clasped hands, spread legs. Play dead.
3. Stay completely still and silent until grizzly leaves.
4. Black bear attack: do NOT play dead — fight back hard, strike nose and eyes.
5. Use bear spray at 8-10 meters as bear charges, aimed at face.

NEVER DO:
- Do NOT run from any bear — you cannot outrun one.
- Do NOT play dead with a black bear — fight back instead.
