Category: Impaled Object

STEPS:
1. Call 911 immediately.
2. Do NOT remove the object — it may be preventing fatal hemorrhage.
3. Build up bulky padding (folded cloths) around both sides to stabilize it.
4. Secure padding with bandages; do not press down on the object.
5. Control bleeding by pressing surrounding tissue, not the object itself.

NEVER DO:
- Do NOT remove the object regardless of its size or location.
- Do NOT press directly on the object.
