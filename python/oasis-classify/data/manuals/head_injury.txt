Category: Head Injury / Concussion

STEPS:
1. Call 911 if unconscious, confused, vomiting repeatedly, unequal pupils, or skull deformity.
2. Keep person still; minimize movement of head and neck.
3. Control scalp bleeding with gentle direct pressure using a clean cloth.
4. If unconscious but breathing, place in recovery position.
5. If not breathing, begin CPR immediately.

NEVER DO:
- Do NOT leave the person alone for at least several hours after injury.
- Do NOT give alcohol, sedatives, or pain medication that can mask symptoms.
