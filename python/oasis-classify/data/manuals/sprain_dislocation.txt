Category: Sprain / Dislocation

STEPS:
1. Stop activity immediately; rest the injured joint.
2. Apply ice wrapped in cloth for 20 minutes to reduce swelling.
3. Apply a compression bandage firmly but not too tightly.
4. Elevate the injured limb above heart level.
5. For dislocation: immobilize in position found; do not attempt to reduce it.

NEVER DO:
- Do NOT attempt to pop a dislocated joint back without medical training.
- Do NOT apply heat in the first 24-48 hours — it increases swelling.
