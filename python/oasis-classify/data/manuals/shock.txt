Category: Shock

STEPS:
1. Call 911 immediately.
2. Treat the underlying cause if possible — apply direct pressure to severe bleeding.
3. Lay the person flat on their back.
4. Elevate the legs 20-30 cm unless injury to head, spine, chest, or lower limbs prevents this.
5. Keep the person warm with a blanket to prevent heat loss.
6. Loosen any tight clothing at the neck, chest, and waist.
7. Reassure the person and keep them still and calm.
8. Monitor breathing and pulse frequently — be prepared to start CPR.

NEVER DO:
- Do NOT give food or drink by mouth.
- Do NOT leave the person alone.
- Do NOT raise the legs if head, chest, or spinal injury is suspected.
- Do NOT allow the person to sit up, walk, or smoke.
