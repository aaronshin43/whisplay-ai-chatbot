Category: Shock

STEPS:
1. Call 911 immediately; treat underlying cause (apply direct pressure to severe bleeding).
2. Lay person flat on their back.
3. Elevate legs 20-30 cm unless head, spine, chest, or lower limb injury prevents it.
4. Keep person warm with a blanket; loosen tight clothing at neck and chest.
5. Monitor breathing and pulse; be prepared to start CPR.

NEVER DO:
- Do NOT raise the legs if head, chest, or spinal injury is suspected.
- Do NOT give food or drink by mouth.
