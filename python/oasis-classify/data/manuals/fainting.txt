Category: Fainting / Syncope

STEPS:
1. If the person feels faint, have them lie down or sit with head between knees before they fall.
2. If they have fainted, lay them on their back on a flat surface.
3. Elevate their legs 20-30 cm to improve blood flow to the brain.
4. Loosen any tight clothing at the neck, chest, and waist.
5. Check breathing and pulse — if not breathing normally, begin CPR.
6. Most people recover within a few minutes; speak reassuringly as they regain consciousness.
7. Do not let them stand up quickly — sit up slowly when fully alert.
8. Call emergency services if unconscious more than 1-2 minutes, there is a head injury, or the person has chest pain.

NEVER DO:
- Do NOT give food or drink until the person is fully conscious and alert.
- Do NOT allow the person to stand immediately after fainting.
- Do NOT slap or splash water aggressively on their face.
- Do NOT dismiss recurrent fainting or fainting with chest pain — seek medical evaluation.
