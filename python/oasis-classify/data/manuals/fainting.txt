Category: Fainting / Syncope

STEPS:
1. If person feels faint, have them lie down or sit with head between knees.
2. If fainted, lay on back and elevate legs 20-30 cm to improve brain blood flow.
3. Loosen tight clothing at neck, chest, and waist.
4. Check breathing — if not breathing normally, begin CPR.
5. Call 911 if unconscious more than 1-2 minutes or person has chest pain.

NEVER DO:
- Do NOT give food or drink until person is fully conscious and alert.
- Do NOT allow person to stand immediately after fainting.
