Category: CPR / Cardiac Arrest

STEPS:
1. Confirm unresponsiveness; call 911 immediately.
2. Tilt head back, lift chin, check breathing for no more than 10 seconds.
3. Place heel of hand on center of chest; compress at least 5 cm deep.
4. Compress at 100-120 per minute; give 30 compressions then 2 rescue breaths.
5. Use AED as soon as available — turn on and follow all voice prompts.

NEVER DO:
- Do NOT stop compressions repeatedly to check for a pulse.
- Do NOT compress too slowly or shallowly — depth and rate are critical for blood flow.
