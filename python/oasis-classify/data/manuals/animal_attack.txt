Category: Wild Animal Attack

STEPS:
1. Do NOT run — running triggers predator chase response.
2. Stand tall, raise arms, open jacket wide; group with others.
3. Speak loudly and firmly; shout to appear threatening.
4. Mountain lion attack: fight back immediately — strike nose, eyes, throat.
5. After attack: control bleeding and call 911 for medical help.

NEVER DO:
- Do NOT run from or turn your back on a mountain lion, wolf, or coyote.
- Do NOT play dead with a mountain lion — fight back aggressively.
