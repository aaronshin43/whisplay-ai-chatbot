Category: Poisonous Plant Exposure

STEPS:
1. Skin contact (ivy, oak, sumac): wash with soap and water immediately — within 10 minutes greatly reduces reaction.
2. Remove contaminated clothing without touching its outer surface.
3. Apply cool compresses; use calamine lotion or hydrocortisone cream for itching.
4. For plant or berry ingestion: call 911 and identify the plant if possible.
5. Eye exposure: flush with clean water for 15-20 minutes without rubbing.

NEVER DO:
- Do NOT induce vomiting after plant ingestion without professional guidance.
- Do NOT burn poison ivy — inhaling the smoke causes severe airway injury.
