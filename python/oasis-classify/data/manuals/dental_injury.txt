Category: Dental Injury

STEPS:
1. Handle knocked-out tooth by crown only — do not touch the root.
2. Rinse gently with milk or saline if dirty — do not scrub.
3. Reinsert tooth into socket immediately if person is conscious; bite down on cloth.
4. If unable to reinsert, store in milk or inside person's cheek.
5. See dentist or emergency room within 30-60 minutes — survival drops after 1 hour.

NEVER DO:
- Do NOT scrub the tooth root or let it dry out.
- Do NOT store the tooth in plain tap water — it damages root cells.
