Category: Heart Attack

STEPS:
1. Call 911 immediately.
2. Help the person sit in a comfortable position — typically sitting upright or semi-reclined reduces heart strain.
3. Loosen any tight clothing around the neck and chest.
4. If the person is not allergic and is conscious, give 300 mg aspirin to chew slowly (not swallow whole).
5. Keep the person calm and still — unnecessary movement increases heart workload.
6. If prescribed nitroglycerin is available and blood pressure is not low, assist with one dose.
7. Monitor breathing and pulse continuously.
8. If the person loses consciousness and stops breathing, begin CPR immediately.

NEVER DO:
- Do NOT leave the person alone.
- Do NOT let the person walk around or exert themselves.
- Do NOT give aspirin if the person is allergic or has been told not to take it.
- Do NOT delay calling 911 hoping symptoms will pass.
