Category: Heart Attack

STEPS:
1. Call 911 immediately.
2. Help person sit in a comfortable semi-reclined position; loosen tight clothing.
3. Give 300 mg aspirin to chew slowly if not allergic and conscious.
4. Keep person calm and still — movement increases heart workload.
5. If person loses consciousness and stops breathing, begin CPR immediately.

NEVER DO:
- Do NOT let the person walk around or exert themselves.
- Do NOT delay calling 911 hoping symptoms will pass.
