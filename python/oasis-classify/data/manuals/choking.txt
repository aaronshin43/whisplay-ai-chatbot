Category: Choking / Airway Obstruction

STEPS:
1. If cannot speak, cough, or breathe, act immediately.
2. Give 5 firm back blows between shoulder blades.
3. If back blows fail: fist just below ribcage above navel, thrust inward and upward.
4. Alternate 5 back blows with 5 abdominal thrusts until expelled.
5. If unconscious, lower to floor, begin CPR, check airway each breath.

NEVER DO:
- Do NOT perform a blind finger sweep -- remove object only if clearly visible.
- Do NOT give abdominal thrusts to infants -- use back blows and chest thrusts.
