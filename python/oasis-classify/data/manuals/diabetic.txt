Category: Diabetic Emergency

STEPS:
1. If conscious and able to swallow, give 15-20 g fast-acting sugar immediately.
2. Use glucose tablets, juice, or sugary drink; have person sit and rest.
3. Repeat sugar if no improvement after 15 minutes.
4. After improvement, give complex carbohydrate snack to maintain blood sugar.
5. Call 911 if unconscious, cannot swallow, or does not improve.

NEVER DO:
- Do NOT give food or drink to an unconscious person.
- Do NOT give insulin without a blood glucose reading.
