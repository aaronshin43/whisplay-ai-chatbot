Category: Eye Injury

STEPS:
1. For chemical splash: flush eye with large amounts of clean water for 20 minutes.
2. Tilt head so affected eye is lower to prevent contaminating the other eye.
3. For foreign body: do not rub — blink rapidly or flush with clean water.
4. If object is embedded or eye is impaled, cover gently with clean cup or cone.
5. Cover both eyes with clean dry dressing to reduce eye movement; seek care.

NEVER DO:
- Do NOT rub the eye when a foreign body is present.
- Do NOT attempt to remove an embedded or impaled object from the eye.
