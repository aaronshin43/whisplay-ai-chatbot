Category: Electric Shock / Electrocution

STEPS:
1. Do NOT touch until power source is confirmed off — you will also be electrocuted.
2. Call 911 immediately; turn off power at main switch or circuit breaker.
3. If power cannot be cut, use dry non-conductive object to push source away.
4. Once safe to approach, check for responsiveness and breathing.
5. If not breathing, begin CPR — cardiac arrest is common after electric shock.

NEVER DO:
- Do NOT touch the person until the power source is off.
- Do NOT use wet or metal objects to move a live electrical source.
