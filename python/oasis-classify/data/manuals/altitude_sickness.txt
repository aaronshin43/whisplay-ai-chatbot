Category: Altitude Sickness

STEPS:
1. Stop ascending and rest if symptoms are mild: headache, nausea, fatigue.
2. Descend immediately if confusion, loss of coordination, or breathlessness at rest.
3. Descend at least 500-1000 meters — most effective treatment.
4. Give supplemental oxygen if available at 2-4 liters per minute.
5. Dexamethasone 8 mg for severe AMS or HACE; Nifedipine 30 mg for HAPE.

NEVER DO:
- Do NOT continue ascending while experiencing any altitude sickness symptoms.
- Do NOT take sleeping pills — they suppress breathing and worsen hypoxia.
