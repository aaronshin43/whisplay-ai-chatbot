Category: Hypothermia

STEPS:
1. Call 911 if person is severely hypothermic, unresponsive, or not breathing.
2. Move to a warm dry location; remove wet clothing and replace with dry layers.
3. Cover including head with blankets; rewarm core first.
4. Apply warm packs wrapped in cloth to neck, armpits, and groin.
5. If unresponsive and not breathing, begin CPR — a cold heart can still be resuscitated.

NEVER DO:
- Do NOT rub limbs — cold blood from extremities can trigger cardiac arrest.
- Do NOT give alcohol — it causes further heat loss.
