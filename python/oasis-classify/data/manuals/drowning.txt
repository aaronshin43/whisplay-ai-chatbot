Category: Drowning / Near-Drowning

STEPS:
1. Ensure your safety — throw rope or extend branch from shore; call 911.
2. Once on shore, check responsiveness.
3. If not breathing, begin rescue breathing: give 5 initial rescue breaths.
4. Use head tilt chin lift to open airway, then start CPR at 30:2 ratio.
5. If breathing, place in recovery position on side; keep person warm.

NEVER DO:
- Do NOT attempt to drain water from lungs before CPR — begin breathing support immediately.
- Do NOT assume person is safe after rescue — secondary drowning can occur hours later.
