Category: Abdominal Injury

STEPS:
1. Call 911 immediately.
2. Lay person on back, knees slightly bent to relax abdominal muscles.
3. If organs are protruding, cover with clean cloth moistened with clean water.
4. Cover moist dressing loosely with plastic wrap to retain moisture.
5. Keep person warm to prevent shock.

NEVER DO:
- Do NOT push protruding organs back inside.
- Do NOT give food, drink, or oral medication before surgery.
